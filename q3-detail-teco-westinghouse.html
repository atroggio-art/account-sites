What They Do

World-leading manufacturer of large and custom electric motors and generators, motor controls, drives, and engineering services — plus large-motor repair. Legacy traces back to Westinghouse; current JV heritage with TECO of Taiwan. Serves petrochemical, utility, pulp & paper, water, mining, and metals markets. Expanded via Motovario (gear reducers/power transmission) and a new Monterrey, Mexico plant to reduce lead times on stock motors. Also pushing a green-energy line including BESS, heat recovery systems, and EV chargers.
Two Business Motions
Both an OEM/large-project business (custom-engineered motors, complex quotes) and a stock-motor/parts business (repeat buyers, distributors). Two distinct motions — both addressable by Salesforce.
