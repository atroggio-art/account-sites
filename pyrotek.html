Why MC Next + Data Cloud
Pyrotek is a global industrial engineering company — nearly 3,000 employees, operations across multiple continents, serving aluminum, glass, and foundry manufacturers with highly technical products and services. Their buyer relationships are long, complex, and deeply technical. Marketing Cloud Next gives Pyrotek's commercial team the tools to stay present across that long buying cycle — automated account-based content, technical nurture sequences, and event-driven communications that reflect where each customer is in their project lifecycle. Data Cloud unifies Pyrotek's global customer data — purchase history, service interactions, regional account data — into profiles that make every customer conversation more informed and more relevant. For a company of this size and complexity, that's a meaningful competitive advantage.
