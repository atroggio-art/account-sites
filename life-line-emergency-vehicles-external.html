Our Point of View

Your Sales Cycle Is 12 Months Long.
Your Marketing Shouldn't Go Quiet for 11 of Them.

EMS agencies and municipalities don't buy on impulse — they budget years in advance, go through committee approvals, and run formal RFP processes. The manufacturers who win are the ones who stay visible, credible, and helpful during that entire window.

"In a spec-driven, relationship-first market, the manufacturer that stays in front of the buyer between trade shows wins the RFP."

Life Line's reputation is built on product quality and dealer relationships. Marketing Cloud Engagement turns that reputation into a systematic advantage — automated nurture sequences, dealer communications, post-sale service touchpoints, and re-order campaigns that run in the background while your team focuses on closing.
