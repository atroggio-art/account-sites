Account Snapshot

What They Do
Ontic licenses, manufactures, and supports legacy and obsolescence-threatened aerospace parts — serving civil airlines, defense primes, and naval operators globally. Their model is essentially: acquire licenses for parts that OEMs no longer want to support, then manufacture and support them at scale. ~600 employees across Chatsworth CA, UK, and global program offices.
Commercial Motion
Multiple BD directors and Technical Sales Managers run separate civil, military, naval, and fuel-systems program tracks simultaneously. Revenue Management (Hakop Karapetyan's team) tracks contract performance separately. This fragmentation is the opening — there's no single view of what's been sold, what's in renewal, and where new BD is happening.
Why MFG Cloud Over Sales Cloud
Sales Cloud already lost in 2024. MFG Cloud's account-based forecasting, run rates, and program-level visibility are differentiated from what they evaluated. Frame this as a purpose-built manufacturing commercial platform, not another CRM pitch.
PE-Backed Context
Ontic was sold to a private investor group (noted in 2020 opp). PE-backed companies are under pressure to show operational leverage — a unified commercial platform that reduces headcount/manual work on forecasting and BD coordination is a compelling CFO story. Bill Nolan (CFO) is worth getting to.
