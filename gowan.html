Why Revenue Cloud Advanced
Gowan sells crop protection products through a complex channel — distributors, dealers, and direct grower relationships — each with different pricing, contracts, and renewal cycles. Revenue Cloud Advanced automates the quote-to-cash process across these channels: CPQ for accurate field quotes, contract management for multi-year deals, and automated billing that removes manual steps their finance team currently owns. For an ag company with seasonal complexity, that's the difference between closing deals before planting season or losing them to a competitor who can move faster.

Account Snapshot

What They Do
Gowan champions crop protection and AgTech solutions for US agriculture and horticulture. Sells through distributors, dealers, and direct grower channels across North America.
Strategic Priority
Scaling their commercial operation and improving speed-to-quote across a distributed field sales team. Reducing manual processes in their quote-to-cash cycle.
Why Salesforce Now
Agricultural sales is highly seasonal — if you can't quote accurately and contract quickly, you lose deals to the spring planting window. RCA removes that friction.
