Product
Loyalty Management
Why Loyalty Management
Nexeo Plastics distributes to thousands of manufacturers and processors who have ongoing, repeat purchasing needs — but no formal loyalty or incentive structure to retain them. Loyalty Management gives Nexeo a platform to build tiered programs that reward high-volume buyers, reduce churn to competitors, and turn transactional relationships into strategic ones. With $3.9B in revenue and a national distribution footprint, even modest improvements in repeat purchase rates translate directly to margin.
🏢

Account Snapshot

What They Do
One of North America's largest plastics distributors — serving manufacturers across automotive, packaging, healthcare, and industrial markets. National footprint with a digital-first commercial model.
Strategic Priority
Deepening customer relationships and growing share of wallet with existing buyers. Digital transformation of their sales and customer experience layer.
Why Salesforce Now
Distribution businesses live and die by repeat purchasing. With pressure from Amazon Business and large competitors, Nexeo needs a programmatic way to reward loyalty and reduce switching risk.
