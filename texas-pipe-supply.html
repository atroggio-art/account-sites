Account Snapshot

What They Do
Texas Pipe & Supply is one of the largest distributors of pipes, valves, fittings, and related products in the US. Serves oil & gas, petrochemical, industrial, and midstream markets from multiple branch locations across Texas and beyond. Family-owned and operated, with a strong culture of relationship-driven sales.
Why MFG Cloud at Scale
A 200-seat MFG Cloud deployment means this is an enterprise-wide go-to-market transformation — standardizing how the entire sales org tracks accounts, manages opportunities, and forecasts across branches. The ROI story is pipeline visibility, forecast accuracy, and reduced revenue leakage from untracked accounts.
Oil & Gas Distribution Context
Distribution in energy is highly cyclical and relationship-driven. Long-standing customer relationships, complex multi-branch account management, and price-sensitive negotiation are the norm. MFG Cloud solves the account visibility and forecasting problem across a distributed, large-scale sales team.
Expansion Path
200-seat MFG Cloud is the land. Service Cloud (for order management and customer service), Revenue Cloud (quoting at scale), and Data Cloud (customer analytics across branches) are the natural follow-ons.
