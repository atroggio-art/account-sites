Product
Commerce Cloud Advanced
Why Commerce Cloud Advanced
FloWorks operates 10 business units across 20 distribution locations in 9 countries — a B2B commerce operation of significant complexity. Their buyers (industrial operators, engineers, procurement teams) increasingly expect the same digital purchasing experience they get from Amazon Business: self-serve catalogs, real-time inventory, contract pricing, and fast reorder. Commerce Cloud Advanced gives FloWorks the platform to deliver that at scale — a branded digital storefront with personalized pricing per account, seamless integration with their ERP, and the ability to scale across business units without rebuilding from scratch. At $175K, this is the highest-value opportunity in the portfolio and the one that most directly positions Salesforce as a strategic platform rather than a point solution.
