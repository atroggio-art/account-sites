What They Do

North America's largest marketer and distributor of fly ash and cement alternatives — the leading supplier of Supplementary Cementitious Materials (SCMs). Recycles ~7M tons of fly ash and ~3M tons of synthetic gypsum annually across 100+ sites in ~45 states. Proprietary Green Cement technology turns industrial byproducts into near-zero-carbon substitutes for portland cement. Serves 4,000–6,000 customer locations through a national terminal, rail, and truck network.
