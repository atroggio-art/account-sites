Why MCA + Data Cloud
Wholesale Electric distributes to contractors, industrial buyers, and utility operators — a customer base that buys frequently, on relationship and price, but rarely receives proactive value-add communication from their suppliers. Marketing Cloud Advanced gives WES the ability to run targeted campaigns across their buyer segments: project-based contractors getting re-engaged after a job, industrial accounts receiving product education and cross-sell, and new account acquisition through digital channels. Data Cloud unifies their customer purchase history, account data, and engagement signals into a single profile — enabling the kind of personalized outreach that turns a transactional distributor relationship into a preferred partner status. At $750M in revenue, the scale of the opportunity here is significant.
