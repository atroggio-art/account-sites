The Deal Story
Pain Reinke Manufacturing — the world's largest center-pivot irrigation equipment manufacturer — runs its dealer and distribution relationships without a purpose-built platform for managing complex channel networks.
Consequence They evaluated a solution last year and walked away, which means the underlying problems are still unsolved while their dealer network grows in complexity and competitive alternatives have had time to improve.
Solution Manufacturing Cloud gives Reinke a dealer channel management platform built for their scale — 125 seats across a global ag distribution network, with the account and run-rate visibility that a market leader needs.
