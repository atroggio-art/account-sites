What We Heard

LS Electric America
Andy Urda, VP Marketing
🧩
Fragmented Stack
HubSpot + ZoomInfo + Power Automate + SharePoint — stitched together, not integrated. Too many logins, too much manual glue.
"I feel like I'm Jeff Bezos starting Amazon… 13 different software programs."
⏱️
Speed to Lead
Qualified leads go stale in the nurture queue. Andy wants a real person on it fast — automated warming is too slow for high-intent signals.
"Fresh bread out of the oven goes stale. The window closes."
🔀
Rep Routing Gap
Hybrid org of direct + independent reps. HubSpot can't route leads to non-seated reps — the #1 reason they moved to HubSpot and it still isn't solved.
"Guess what? That's not exactly true today."
🤖
AI Cross-Sell Signal
Wants AI to mine ERP history and surface: "This customer buys X — here are 3 similar accounts that don't. Go talk to them."
"That's marketing intelligence I could actually use."
📄
Inbound PO Automation
Customers email PDF purchase orders. New portal should read the PDF, populate the form, and auto-flag missing fields — no human touchpoint needed.
"We did OCR 20 years ago at Yaskawa. This isn't new."
📈
Scale Demands It
Revenue: $50M → $750M → $1.5B+ this year. Data center boom is accelerating. Manual processes and workarounds won't hold at this growth rate.
"A lot of that growth is into data centers."
