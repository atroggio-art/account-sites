Our Point of View

Selling Through Dealers and Reps Is Complex.
Managing It Shouldn't Be.

Delfield's go-to-market runs through a multi-tier channel: dealers, reps, DSRs, and direct accounts across restaurant, healthcare, education, and hospitality. Manufacturing Cloud gives your entire commercial team a single system to manage that complexity at scale.

"In commercial foodservice, the brands that win are the ones whose reps and dealers have the best information, not the best product alone."

Delfield's products are exceptional. The opportunity is making sure every dealer, every rep, and every sales manager has the same real-time view of accounts, pipeline, and service status. That's how Delfield sells more intelligently, services more proactively, and retains customers more consistently than any competitor.

🤝
Dealer and channel management built into the platform

Manufacturing Cloud's partner relationship management tracks dealer pipeline, activity, and performance in one system. That means Delfield has real visibility into what's happening in the channel without relying on manual reports from reps and distributors.

📊
Run-rate forecasting for a recurring revenue business

Commercial kitchen equipment drives significant replacement and service revenue. MFG Cloud's run-rate agreement tools let Delfield forecast recurring account revenue alongside new business, so leadership gets a complete picture of the commercial pipeline.

🔧
Sales and Service connected in one system

When a sales rep can see open service cases on an account before a call, and a service director can see sales activity, the entire customer relationship improves. MFG Cloud Sales connects directly to Service Cloud for a complete account view.
