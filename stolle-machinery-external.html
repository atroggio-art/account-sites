Our Point of View

You've Built the World's Best Machines.
The Service Business Deserves the Same Investment.

Stolle's installed base is the business. Every machine in the field is a parts relationship, a service contract, and a renewal opportunity. Right now, most of that is being managed manually.

"The most scalable growth Stolle can drive right now isn't in new machine sales. It's in digitizing the aftermarket."

A global parts catalog that customers can order from 24/7. A field service org that dispatches and resolves intelligently. A sales team that knows every account's install footprint before the call. These are solved problems for manufacturers who've made the investment.

🛒
Parts should sell themselves

Your customers know what they need. A self-service B2B Commerce portal lets them order 24/7 without routing through a sales rep. That turns your parts catalog into a scalable, always-on revenue channel.

🔧
Your field engineers are your most expensive resource

FSL + Agentforce gives your service org intelligent scheduling, mobile case access, and AI-driven triage. Your best engineers solve problems, not paperwork.

📊
You're already on Salesforce and the platform is ready

Commerce Cloud and Field Service extend what you've already invested in. No rip-and-replace. No new platform commitment. Just more capability on the system your teams already use.
