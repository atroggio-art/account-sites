The Opportunity

How Advance Polybag Could Know Which Customers Are Ready to Reorder -- Before They Call

Your customers are repeat buyers. The order history, product specs, and timing data to predict the next purchase already exist -- the question is whether your sales team is using it.

What We Noticed
01
Two brands, likely two customer databases. Advance Polybag and Accredo Packaging serve overlapping markets under the same roof. Without a shared customer view, cross-sell opportunities across the portfolio are probably invisible to the people who could act on them.
02
Custom manufacturing creates rich reorder data. Every order comes with specs, volume, and timing. That's a detailed pattern of when each customer runs out -- exactly the kind of signal a proactive outreach program could turn into revenue before a competitor gets the call.
03
Sustainability is a live differentiator. A combined VP of Marketing and Sustainability role signals this is a market message, not just an internal initiative. Consistently delivering that message to existing accounts requires a connected communication layer that most manufacturers don't have.
The Point of View
The biggest opportunity may not be finding new accounts. It may be using the order history and customer data already sitting in your systems to reach existing buyers at exactly the right moment -- and giving reps the visibility to make that call before someone else does.
What This Could Enable
Proactive Reorder Outreach
Trigger outreach to customers based on their historical order cadence -- reaching them during the reorder window rather than waiting for an inbound call that may go to a competitor first.
Cross-Portfolio Visibility
Surface Accredo Packaging opportunities to Advance Polybag reps (and vice versa) based on what a customer already buys -- turning two separate books of business into one connected growth motion.
Rep Visibility Into Account Health
Give every rep a live view of account activity, order history, and engagement signals so they spend time on accounts that are ready to buy -- not on manual research or status calls.
The Next Step

This is based on outside research, so some assumptions may be wrong. Worth 20 minutes to pressure test it?
