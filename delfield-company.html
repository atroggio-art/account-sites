Account Snapshot

What They Do
Delfield manufactures commercial refrigeration and foodservice equipment — reach-in refrigerators, prep tables, chef bases, and custom cold-side solutions for restaurants, healthcare, education, and hospitality. One of the most recognized brands in the foodservice equipment industry.
Why MFG Cloud at 89 Seats
An 89-seat deployment means this is a company-wide CRM initiative — covering the full sales org, dealer/distributor channel, and potentially service. MFG Cloud's run-rate agreements, account-based forecasting, and dealer management are directly relevant to a manufacturer that sells through a multi-tier channel.
Channel Complexity
Foodservice equipment manufacturers sell through dealers, reps, and DSRs (dealer sales reps) — a multi-tier channel that's notoriously hard to manage without a proper CRM. MFG Cloud's partner relationship management capabilities are a key differentiator here.
Parent Company Context
Welbilt (now Ali Group) is the parent. A deal this size may require parent-level approval. If Welbilt has existing Salesforce relationships, that could accelerate — or complicate — the deal. Worth investigating before final close push.
