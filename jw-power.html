Why Marketing Cloud Next
J-W Power sells and services natural gas compression and power equipment to oil & gas operators — a relationship-driven, long-cycle business where the buyer journey can span months. Marketing Cloud Next gives their team the tools to stay relevant across that whole journey: automated nurture sequences for prospects, service reminder campaigns for existing customers, and behavioral intelligence to know when an account is ready for the next conversation. For a company with 850 employees and national field operations, MC Next replaces scattered outreach with a coordinated, data-driven marketing engine.

What They Do
Leading provider of natural gas compression and power generation equipment and services to the oil & gas industry. Sells, rents, and services units across North America.
Strategic Priority
Growing their service and rental revenue base while managing long sales cycles in a market driven by operator relationships and energy price cycles.
