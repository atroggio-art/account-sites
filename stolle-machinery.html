Account Snapshot

What They Do
World's leading supplier of two-piece can and end-making machinery for the global canmaking industry. Customers are major packaging manufacturers (Ball, Crown, Ardagh). Complex capital equipment with a large global installed base — parts and service is a significant revenue stream.
Existing Salesforce Relationship
Active Salesforce customer on 43 Platform, 40 SFA, 81 Org licenses (renewal not until 2029). Not a greenfield — all expansion conversations should frame as building on existing investment, not adding new burden.
Where Commerce Fits
Parts and consumables sales are a natural digital commerce play — global can manufacturers need self-service ordering for replacement parts. Chris Zagrodnichek (VP Global Parts) is the direct business owner for this motion.
Where FSL Fits
Stolle machines require expert on-site service globally. Field Service Lightning + Agentforce gives their service org scheduling intelligence, mobile access, and AI-driven case resolution. Michael Fitzgerald owns this relationship.
