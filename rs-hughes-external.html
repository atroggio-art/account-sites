R.S. Hughes' competitive advantage is its people — experienced reps who know their customers, their products, and their needs. The risk is when that knowledge lives only in people's heads. Manufacturing Cloud captures it in a system — so every handoff is seamless and every account is protected.

"The best distributors win on relationships. Manufacturing Cloud makes sure those relationships survive rep transitions, territory changes, and scale."

When a top rep at R.S. Hughes leaves, their customers shouldn't go with them — but without a CRM, that's exactly what happens. Manufacturing Cloud Sales & Service gives every account a permanent home in the system: contact history, order patterns, service cases, and relationship notes — accessible by any rep, any manager, any time.
