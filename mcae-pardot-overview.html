What is MCAE
B2B marketing automation
built inside Salesforce

MCAE is the only marketing automation platform natively built on Salesforce CRM. Every lead score, every email open, every form fill flows directly into the CRM record — so sales and marketing are always working from the same data.

Who it's for

B2B marketing teams selling to other businesses — particularly those with longer sales cycles, multiple stakeholders, and field sales teams. Ideal for manufacturing, distribution, financial services, and technology companies.

What problem it solves

Marketing sends leads to sales with no context. Reps don't know who's hot, who's cold, or what content they've engaged with. Deals get lost in handoff. MCAE closes that gap by scoring, nurturing, and routing leads with full engagement history attached.

How it connects to Salesforce

MCAE syncs natively with Salesforce CRM. Lead scores update on Contact records in real time. Campaign influence ties marketing activity to closed revenue. Sales and marketing see identical data — no manual exports, no spreadsheet handoffs.

MCAE vs. Marketing Cloud Engagement

MCAE is built for B2B (account-based, longer cycles, sales-aligned). Marketing Cloud Engagement (formerly ExactTarget) is built for B2C (high volume, transactional, consumer journeys). Most manufacturing and distribution companies need MCAE first.

Full Feature Set
Everything inside MCAE

Six capability areas, each solving a different part of the B2B marketing-to-revenue problem.

Drag-and-Drop Email Builder

Build branded emails without code using a visual builder with reusable content blocks, responsive templates, and live preview. Includes mobile optimization.

Drip Programs (Nurture Tracks)

Automated multi-step email sequences triggered by prospect behavior, time delays, or CRM field changes. Each step reacts to what the prospect did — opened, clicked, visited a page, filled a form.

Dynamic Content

Show different content blocks to different audience segments within the same email. An engineer and a VP receive the same campaign with copy and offers tailored to their role and stage.

Send Time Optimization

AI-powered analysis of historical engagement data to automatically send each email at the time each individual prospect is most likely to open it.

1:1 Sales Emails (Salesforce Inbox)

Sales reps send tracked, templated emails directly from Gmail or Outlook through the CRM. Opens, clicks, and replies log automatically to the Contact record.

List Segmentation

Static and dynamic lists based on any combination of CRM fields, behavior history, lead score, industry, job title, or custom criteria. Lists update automatically as prospect data changes.

Prospect Scoring

Automatically assigns numeric scores based on engagement activity — email opens (+5), link clicks (+10), page visits (+3), form fills (+50), pricing page visits (+25). Score reflects interest level.

Prospect Grading

Grades (A–F) measure fit against your ideal customer profile — industry, company size, job title, region. Separate from score: a prospect can be highly engaged (high score) but wrong fit (low grade).

Score Decay

Scores automatically decrease when prospects go inactive. A lead that was hot six months ago and hasn't engaged since won't still appear at the top of the queue — keeping rep focus on current interest.

Einstein Lead Scoring

AI model trained on your own historical data predicts which leads are most likely to convert. Updates dynamically as new engagement data arrives. Surfaced directly on Lead and Contact records in CRM.

Custom Scoring Categories

Score prospects differently by product line or business unit. A prospect interested in Product A gets a separate score from their interest in Product B — letting reps see exactly which solution each prospect cares about.

Score-Triggered Alerts

Notify the assigned rep in real time the moment a prospect crosses a score threshold — or visits a high-intent page like pricing or request-a-quote — so reps follow up while the prospect is actively engaged.

Form Builder

Drag-and-drop form creation with custom field mapping to Salesforce objects. Progressive profiling collects different fields on each visit, building complete records over time without overwhelming prospects on first touch.

Landing Page Builder

Full landing pages built in MCAE with A/B testing, custom domains, and SEO settings. No web team dependency — marketers build, test, and publish campaign pages independently.

Form Handlers

Connect MCAE to existing forms on your website without migrating them. Prospect data submitted through any web form automatically flows into MCAE and syncs to Salesforce.

Personalized URLs (PURLs)

Generate unique, trackable URLs for each prospect. When a prospect visits their PURL, the page pre-fills with their data, tracks the visit to their record, and triggers automation based on that interaction.

Site Search Tracking

When a known prospect searches your website, the search terms log to their MCAE record. Reps see exactly what a prospect was looking for before they called — immediate context for the first conversation.

Social Sign-On

Allow prospects to fill forms using their LinkedIn or other social credentials. Reduces friction on form completion, automatically enriches records with professional data, and increases conversion rates.

Visual Automation Builder

Drag-and-drop canvas for building multi-branch automation programs. Every path is visual — triggers, waits, actions, and decision points are laid out as a flowchart anyone on the team can read and edit.

Trigger Rules

Programs start based on prospect actions (form fill, email click, page visit, list membership) or CRM events (deal stage change, field update, task created). Any combination of criteria can fire a trigger.

Decision Branching

Programs fork based on what a prospect does. Opened the email? Go down path A. Didn't open after 3 days? Path B sends a different subject line. Clicked pricing? Alert the rep immediately.

Action Library

Over 30 automated actions: send email, adjust score, change CRM field, create task, add to list, remove from list, assign to rep, send internal alert, add to Salesforce campaign, and more.

Re-engagement Programs

Automatically identify and re-engage prospects who have gone cold. A pre-built re-engagement track sends a targeted sequence and removes unresponsive prospects from active lists to keep data clean.

Program Testing Mode

Test any automation program against real prospect records before activating it — see exactly what path each prospect would take, what actions would fire, and what they'd receive. No live sends until you approve.

Campaign Influence Reporting

Connect every closed deal back to the marketing touches that influenced it. First-touch, last-touch, and multi-touch attribution models show marketing's contribution to pipeline and closed revenue — not just leads generated.

Lifecycle Reporting

Visualize how prospects move through each stage of your funnel — from anonymous visitor to MQL to SQL to Opportunity to Closed Won. Identify exactly where leads are converting and where they're dropping off.

Email Performance Dashboard

Deliverability, open rate, click-through rate, unsubscribe rate, and bounce rate for every email and campaign. Compare performance across campaigns, time periods, and audience segments in one view.

ROI Reporting

Compare marketing spend against pipeline influenced and revenue generated for each campaign. Executives see exactly which programs are producing revenue — and which ones aren't justifying their budget.

Engagement History on CRM Records

Every email sent, every page visited, every form filled, and every score change appears on the Contact and Account record in Salesforce. Reps see the full marketing history before every call.

B2B Marketing Analytics (Plus)

Advanced Einstein Analytics dashboards for deeper campaign attribution, account-level engagement trends, and cross-channel performance. Available on the Advanced and Premium editions.

Account-Based Engagement

Aggregate engagement scores at the account level — not just the individual contact. See which companies are most engaged across all their stakeholders, not just the one person who opened an email.

Target Account Lists

Sync Salesforce Account lists directly into MCAE to run account-specific campaigns. Every contact at a target account receives coordinated outreach — marketing and sales working the same list in parallel.

Engagement History on Account Records

The Account record in Salesforce shows a rollup of all marketing engagement across every contact at that company — which campaigns touched them, what they clicked, and where they are in the funnel.

Connected Campaigns

Create a Salesforce Campaign once — it automatically creates the corresponding MCAE program. All engagement data flows back to the Campaign record, enabling multi-touch attribution and closed-loop reporting in Salesforce.

Sales + Marketing Alignment Alerts

When a known contact at a target account visits a high-intent page, both marketing and the assigned rep are alerted instantly. No more warm accounts sitting unworked because the rep didn't know they were engaged.

Account Scoring

Custom account-level scores calculated from the combined activity of all contacts at a company. Identify which companies are heating up — even if no single contact has hit the individual scoring threshold.

End-to-End Flow
From anonymous visitor
to closed deal
01

Prospect visits site

MCAE tracking code identifies the visitor. First touch is logged — source, pages viewed, time on site.

02

Form fill / content gate

Prospect downloads content or requests info. Record created in MCAE, synced to Salesforce Lead or Contact.

03

Score builds over time

Every subsequent email open, page visit, and content download adds to the score. Grading checks fit against ICP.

04

Nurture program runs

Engagement Studio sends the right content at the right time based on behavior — no manual marketing required.

05

MQL threshold hit

Score reaches threshold — rep is alerted instantly with full engagement history. Lead converts to MQL in CRM.

06

Opportunity + attribution

Deal closes. Campaign influence reporting ties the win back to every marketing touch that contributed — proving marketing ROI in hard numbers.

Where It Wins
Common use cases in B2B
01

Trade show follow-up at scale

Upload a list of badge scans from a trade show. Every contact automatically enters a 6-email nurture track, gets scored based on engagement, and hot leads get routed to reps within 24 hours — no manual sorting.

02

Rep-triggered nurture

A rep adds a prospect to a campaign after a discovery call. MCAE takes over — sending relevant content over the next 30 days, alerting the rep when the prospect re-engages, and keeping the deal warm without rep effort.

03

Win-back campaigns

Identify customers who haven't purchased in 90+ days. Automatically enroll them in a targeted win-back sequence with relevant offers and case studies. Flag the ones who re-engage for personal rep outreach.

04

New product launch

Segment your database by the product lines they've engaged with. Send highly targeted launch announcements — customers who've asked about industrial solutions get a different message than those interested in consumer products.

05

Lead qualification at scale

Marketing generates 500 inbound leads per month. Without scoring, reps must manually qualify all 500. With MCAE, only the top 15% — those with high scores and strong ICP grades — ever reach a sales rep's queue.

06

Multi-stakeholder account nurture

B2B deals involve 7+ stakeholders. MCAE tracks engagement across all contacts at an account — so sales can see when the CFO, CIO, and VP Operations are all engaging, signaling a committee is forming.
