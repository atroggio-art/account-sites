Account Snapshot

What They Do
R.S. Hughes is one of North America's largest independent distributors of adhesives, tapes, abrasives, safety supplies, and specialty industrial products. Sunnyvale, CA HQ with branches across the US and Mexico. Serves manufacturing, aerospace, electronics, and automotive customers.
Why MFG Cloud Sales & Service
A 50-rep sales organization across inside sales, outside reps, and national accounts needs a unified CRM to track accounts, manage pipeline, log service cases, and forecast accurately. MFG Cloud S&S gives them both the CRM and the service layer in one platform — relevant for a distributor that handles warranty, returns, and post-sale support alongside new business.
Mexico Operations
Alan Belinky (GM Mexico) signals active Mexico operations — a cross-border use case that benefits from a centralized CRM. MFG Cloud's multi-region forecasting and account planning is a differentiator here.
Expansion Path
MFG Cloud S&S is the land. Revenue Cloud (quoting at scale for complex distributor pricing), B2B Commerce (dealer/customer self-service portal), and Marketing Cloud (nurture and reactivation campaigns) are natural follow-ons.
