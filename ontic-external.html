"The most valuable thing a modern CRM does for a company like Ontic isn't tracking contacts. It's giving your commercial team full program visibility so nothing slips between BD and revenue."

When you're running civil, military, naval, and MRO motions simultaneously, the handoff between business development and revenue management is where deals get dropped.
