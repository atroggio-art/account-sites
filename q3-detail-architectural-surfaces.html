The Deal Story
Pain A sale at Architectural Surfaces Group spans architects, designers, contractors, and installers — but their CRM treats every interaction as a standalone contact, not a connected relationship across that lifecycle.
Consequence Sales reps lose visibility when a specification moves from designer to contractor, deals fall through the cracks between showrooms, and competition that tracks the full arc wins the install.
Solution Sales Cloud EE + Service Cloud EE gives ASG a connected view of every relationship from first spec to final installation — across every showroom, channel, and customer type.
📋

Business Problems

  • Complex sales cycles involving architects, designers, and contractors with no unified stakeholder view across the journey

Value Proposition

A unified sales and service platform that gives ASG's team the tools to manage complex, multi-stakeholder customer relationships — from first specification through installation and beyond.

  • Supports multi-channel, multi-location distribution businesses at scale
  • Single unified view across all showrooms, brands, and sales teams
