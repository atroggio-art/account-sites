Our Point of View

A Bigger Catalog Is Only an Advantage
If Your Dealers Can Actually Sell It.

Bad Boy Mowers is building the most complete outdoor power lineup it's ever had. New facility. New categories. New price tiers. The challenge isn't production. It's dealer enablement. A dealer who can configure a tractor package, quote it accurately, and submit the order without calling anyone sells more of it. A dealer who needs a rep on the phone to do any of that sells less and costs more to serve.

"Every new SKU you add to the lineup is an ordering complexity your dealer network has to absorb. A portal handles that at scale. A rep on the phone doesn't."

Bad Boy is expanding from a focused mower lineup into tractors, UTVs, light construction equipment, and outdoor power tools. That's multiple product categories, multiple buyer personas, and multiple order types. residential, commercial, and contractor. all hitting the dealer channel at once.

A B2B dealer portal built around your catalog lets every dealer configure and quote any product accurately, see real-time availability from the new facility, and submit a clean order without picking up the phone. Your sales team focuses on relationships and growth. Not order entry and pricing disputes.

01
New facility means new SKUs hitting the channel fast

The Monroeville tractor plant opens early 2026. That's new inventory, new configurations, and new pricing hitting your dealer network on a tight timeline. A portal connected to your production and inventory data means dealers see what's available from day one, with no lag and no calls to check availability on a new model.

02
Complex product lines need guided configuration, not guesswork

Sub-compact 10 series to powerful 50 series tractors. Commercial mowers. UTVs. Construction equipment. A dealer who doesn't sell tractors every day needs a guided configure-price-quote tool to get the right package for their customer. Without it, you get wrong orders, margin disputes, and returns.

03
Multiple buyer personas means multiple selling motions

Commercial workhorses and residential favorites don't sell the same way. A landscaping company buying a 50-series tractor fleet has a different process than a homeowner buying a Rebel SD. A portal that segments by buyer type and routes commercial quotes to the right rep gives your dealers the right tool for every conversation.

04
Dealer performance visibility you don't have today

Which dealers are selling the new tractor lineup? Which ones are stuck on mowers because that's what they know? Right now you likely don't have clean data to answer those questions fast. A connected dealer portal gives you real-time sell-through data, activation rates by SKU, and flags the dealers who need enablement before the busy season starts.
