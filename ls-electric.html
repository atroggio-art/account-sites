Account Snapshot

What They Do
LS Electric America is the North American subsidiary of LS ELECTRIC CO., LTD. (Korea, $2.3B global revenue). They manufacture and sell power systems, automation products, and electrical components — switchgear, drives, PLCs, EV charging infrastructure, energy storage systems. Sell through a mix of direct reps, distributors, and channel partners across industrial, HVAC, energy, and utility segments.
The Growth Problem
US revenue is ~$20M. The goal is $100M in 3–5 years — a 5x growth target with only 3 sales reps in North America. That math only works with automation, account-based marketing, and digital prospecting infrastructure. They can't just hire their way to $100M. They need a system that makes every rep do the work of ten.
The Forcing Functions
Two platform decisions happening simultaneously: (1) HubSpot marketing EOL March 2027 — they must migrate. (2) ZoomInfo contract expiring in ~4 months — they're evaluating replacements. MCA + Data Cloud answers both. Don't let them solve these as separate decisions with two separate vendors.
Korean Context
Parent company in Korea has a Salesforce relationship and is doing a global rollout. This is both an opportunity (internal mandate helps) and a risk (Korean procurement politics can delay or override local decisions). Stay close to Andy Urda and Eric Chung at the US level to drive momentum independently.
