Account Snapshot

What They Do
Life Line Emergency Vehicles manufactures custom ambulances and emergency response vehicles. Headquartered in Sumner, Iowa. Sells primarily to EMS agencies, municipalities, fire departments, and hospitals across the US. Custom-built to spec with long lead times — a relationship-driven, high-stakes B2B sale.
Why Marketing Cloud
Their go-to-market relies on dealers, direct relationships, and trade shows. Marketing Cloud Engagement gives them a professional platform to manage dealer newsletters, prospect nurture, post-sale service communications, and re-order reminders — turning ad-hoc outreach into consistent, automated programs.
Sales Motion
Long sales cycles (6–18 months for fleet purchases), committee-based buying decisions at the agency/municipality level, and a mix of direct and dealer-channel sales. Marketing automation helps stay top-of-mind during the long evaluation window.
Expansion Path
MC Engagement is the entry point. Data Cloud unification and Sales Cloud for dealer pipeline management are natural follow-ons once the platform is embedded.
