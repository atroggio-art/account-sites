The Opportunity

You Built the Foundation.
The Dealer Ordering Layer Is the Missing Piece.

Demco has Sales Cloud, Maps, MuleSoft connected to Epicor, and a team that knows how to run on Salesforce. Everything needed to give dealers a world-class ordering and quoting experience already exists -- it just hasn't been connected to the front door yet.

What We See in the Work So Far
01
The Epicor integration is the unlock. MuleSoft already connects Salesforce to your ERP. That means product data, pricing, and inventory are accessible in real time -- exactly what a dealer portal needs to show accurate availability and generate a valid quote without a rep in the middle.
02
A standalone webstore keeps failing for a reason. Past attempts to bolt on a web ordering tool didn't stick. The problem isn't the idea -- it's that a tool outside the Salesforce environment creates a new integration to maintain and a data silo to manage. The solution has to be native to what you've already built.
03
Complex configurations are a daily friction point. Grain carts, sprayers, and trailers ship in hundreds of valid configurations. Every dealer order that requires a phone call to confirm fitment or pricing is a rep hour spent on something the system should handle -- and a quote that may contain an error no one catches until it's too late.
The Point of View
The dealer portal isn't a new project -- it's the natural next layer on the platform you already run on. The right approach isn't to add another tool. It's to extend what already works, using the data that's already in Salesforce, so dealers can self-serve and your team stops spending time on orders that shouldn't need a human touch.
What This Could Enable
A Dealer Portal Native to Salesforce
Dealers log in, see their account-specific pricing and catalog, place orders against real-time Epicor inventory, and track status without calling a rep. Built on your existing Salesforce environment -- no new integration to maintain, no separate system to keep in sync.
Guided Configuration That Eliminates Mis-Quotes
Configuration rules built into the quoting layer mean dealers can only build valid combinations. No more calls to confirm fitment. No more orders that come back because the spec was wrong. And every quote is generated from the same pricing logic your sales team already uses.
Revenue Visibility Across the Dealer Base
When dealer orders flow through Salesforce, your sales team finally gets a live view of which accounts are growing, which are slowing, and where there's whitespace in the product assortment -- turning the order data into a signal your reps can act on.
The Next Step

We have a dealer portal conversation already started. This is the thinking behind where it should go -- and why building on what you already own is the right path forward.
