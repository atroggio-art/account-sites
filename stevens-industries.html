Why MCA + Data Cloud
Stevens Industries is a custom manufacturer with 40 sales reps across 3 different sales groups — and a challenge that's common in that structure: tracking customer contacts, managing prospect interactions, and preventing deals from falling through the cracks when a rep changes territory or leaves. Marketing Cloud Advanced gives Stevens an automated outreach layer that keeps their prospects and customers engaged between rep touchpoints. Data Cloud connects their customer interaction history, ERP order data, and sales activity into a unified record — giving management the visibility they need and reducing the duplication with their existing ERP. For a company already aware of the CRM coordination problem, the question isn't whether to solve it — it's how.
