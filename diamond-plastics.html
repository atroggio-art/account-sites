Why MCA + Data Cloud
Diamond Plastics is one of the largest PVC pipe producers in the world — serving agriculture, utilities, and industry from plants across 7 states. Their buyers are engineers, procurement managers, and project contractors who operate on project cycles and bid-driven timelines. Marketing Cloud Advanced gives Diamond a way to stay in front of those buyers between projects: targeted communications tied to procurement cycles, product education for new sizes and applications, and dealer and distributor enablement campaigns. Data Cloud unifies buyer data across regional plants and sales teams — enabling national visibility into account health and a coordinated marketing motion that today operates plant-by-plant.
