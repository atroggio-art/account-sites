Account Snapshot

What They Do
Nekter Juice Bar is a fast-casual health and wellness franchise with 100+ locations across the US. Known for cold-pressed juices, smoothies, and acai bowls. Founded by Steve and Alexis Schulze — a co-founder brand with strong identity and loyal customer base.
Why Loyalty Management
Franchise brands live on repeat visits and word of mouth. Without a loyalty program, Nekter can't measure which customers are churning, can't personalize offers by behavior, and can't build the kind of relationship data that drives long-term LTV. Loyalty Management Starter connects purchase data to individual profiles and feeds Marketing Cloud for automated, personalized comms.
Marketing Cloud Angle
The Loyalty play opens a larger Marketing Cloud conversation — email, SMS, journey automation, and Data Cloud unification across franchise locations. Start with Loyalty as the entry point.
Franchise Complexity
With franchisees operating independently, there's likely inconsistency in how customer data is collected and used across locations. A centralized loyalty platform solves the coordination problem at the franchisor level while giving individual franchisees visibility.
