Why MCA + Data Cloud
Smead has been organizing offices for over a century — they have deep brand recognition and broad retail distribution (Office Depot, Amazon, resellers). But in a digitally-driven retail landscape, brand awareness alone doesn't drive repeat purchase. Marketing Cloud Advanced gives Smead the tools to reach their buyers — procurement leads, office managers, and retail partners — with targeted, automated campaigns that move them through the funnel. Data Cloud unifies Smead's customer data across retail channels, direct relationships, and resellers into one profile, enabling smarter segmentation and personalization at scale. For a new logo, this bundle is the entry point to a full Salesforce platform conversation.
