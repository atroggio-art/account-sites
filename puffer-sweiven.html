Product
Order Management System (OMS)
Why OMS
As the largest Emerson Impact Partner in the US, Puffer-Sweiven handles complex, multi-line orders across process control equipment, instrumentation, and project services — often across multiple subsidiaries and delivery timelines. Their current order management is fragmented across systems, creating manual work and visibility gaps between sales, operations, and customers. Salesforce OMS unifies order capture, fulfillment tracking, and exception management in one platform — giving their inside sales and operations teams a single source of truth and enabling the kind of proactive customer communication that builds long-term relationships in a relationship-driven industry.
