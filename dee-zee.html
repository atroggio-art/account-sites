Why MCA + Data Cloud
Dee Zee makes truck accessories — toolboxes, bed rails, steps, fuel tanks — for a consumer base that's passionate, brand-loyal, and heavily influenced by digital content. Their buyers research on YouTube, purchase through dealers and e-commerce, and engage with OEM and aftermarket brands on social. Marketing Cloud Advanced enables Dee Zee to orchestrate personalized marketing across these channels: retargeting truck owners after purchase, running dealer co-op campaigns, and automating lifecycle campaigns tied to product registrations. Data Cloud unifies their consumer data across retail, e-commerce, and dealer channels — so marketing can target and personalize based on what someone actually bought, not just who they are.
